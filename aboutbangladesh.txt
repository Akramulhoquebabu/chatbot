Is Bangladesh rich or poor?
Bangladesh is a developing nation. Despite rapid economic growth, poverty remains major issue. However, poverty has declined sharply in recent history. Shortly after its independence, approximately 90% of the population lived under the poverty line.

What is Bangladesh famous food?
Rice with Curry, Vorta, Vaji, and Daal – Everyday Bangladeshi food. Plain rice is the main food in Bangladesh. It is served with different fish and meat curry, Bhorta (mash) of different vegetables and fish, bhaji (fried) of different vegetables, and Patla Daal (lentil soup).
